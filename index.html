What is myrical?

< music + lyrical = myrical

mission statement:

< myrical exists to provide aspiring musicians without access, the tools and skills, necessary to learn music through technology. >

vision statement:

< myrical will provide those in disadvantaged communities, an experience of joy and peace that music can bring. myrical will provide an avenue of learning instruments other than piano, guitar, and bass. the app will start with providing, all communities the opportunity to learn music, and will then progress to communities that are not fortunate enough to have arts education. >
